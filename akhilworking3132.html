About Our School

“LEAD INDIA BHARAT RATNAS SCHOOL” was established by Dr. N.B. Sudershan Acharya a renowned educationalist and humanist, to fill the gaps in education for the holistic development of students with Values & 21st Century Life Skills. The school was inaugurated by Ex-President and Bharat Ratna Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam 0n 21-06-13. Dr. N.B. Sudershan Acharya, the Founder Chairman of Lead India Bharat Ratnas (LIBR) School is a great visionary, renowned educationalist and humanist with international acclaim. He was awarded Vidhya Marthanda, “Eminent Citizen of India”.
